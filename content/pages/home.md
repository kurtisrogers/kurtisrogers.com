## introduction

I'm an experienced full-stack software engineer based in **Bristol, UK**, with more than ten years building for agencies, product teams, and public-sector clients.

I specialise in **Node.js and Express** on the server, **React and modern JavaScript** on the front end, and **accessible, user-centred interfaces** that hold up under real-world scrutiny. Recent work spans justice-sector software at [Unilink](https://www.unilink.com), product delivery with Remix and MongoDB at TeamTech4, and three years as a senior frontend developer at Nomensa (GAIN) — shipping solutions for clients from startups to government.

I'm equally comfortable in greenfield builds, component libraries, and long-lived codebases that need careful refactoring. I care about **test-driven development**, clear documentation, and code that the next developer can pick up with confidence.

## honourableDeveloper

Good engineering is about more than shipping features. Colleagues describe me as approachable, positive, and generous with my time — whether that's pairing on a tricky UI problem, reviewing a pull request, or mentoring less experienced developers.

I'm a firm believer in **web accessibility** as a baseline, not a bonus. I follow standards, test with real assistive technology where it matters, and keep a close eye on emerging CSS and HTML capabilities that make inclusive design easier to deliver.

I work well with designers, product owners, and stakeholders at every level: translating requirements into sensible technical choices, communicating trade-offs clearly, and staying pragmatic when deadlines are tight.

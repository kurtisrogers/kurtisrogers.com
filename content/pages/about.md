## technology

My stack has evolved with the industry, but the through-line is **JavaScript and TypeScript** across the full stack — from server-rendered and SPA front ends to APIs and integrations.

**Languages & runtimes:** TypeScript, JavaScript, Node.js, PHP (WordPress and legacy e-commerce)

**Front end:** React, Remix, SolidJS, HTML email, responsive design, component libraries (including Ant Design), design-system thinking

**Back end & data:** Express, Koa, MongoDB, Strapi, REST APIs, AWS

**Quality & delivery:** Test-driven development, Cypress end-to-end testing, Large-Scale Scrum (LeSS), performance and bandwidth optimisation

**Focus areas:** Web accessibility (WCAG), semantic HTML, progressive enhancement, and practical use of AI tooling in modern development workflows

I'm always learning. Recent interests include AI-assisted development, CSS features like `contrast-color()`, and spec-driven UI work — knowing the right component vocabulary makes both human and agent collaboration sharper.

## career

I've spent my career in and around **Bristol's digital sector**, growing from junior WordPress and e-commerce roles into senior frontend and full-stack positions.

**Unilink** — *Software Engineer* (2026–present)  
Building software for probation and prison services across the UK and internationally. Justice-sector technology at scale, with high standards for reliability and security.

**TeamTech4** — *Web Developer* (2025)  
Delivered product work with **Remix, Express, and MongoDB**. Practised end-to-end test-driven development with Cypress in a fast-moving consultancy environment.

**Nomensa (GAIN)** — *Senior Frontend Developer* (2022–2025)  
Three years shipping JavaScript solutions for a wide range of clients, from commercial brands to government. Progressed from frontend developer to senior, with a focus on accessible, well-tested interfaces.

**Bigg** — *Software Developer* (2019–2020)  
WordPress themes and plugins, plus an in-house React tool with Ant Design and an AWS backend.

**Earlier roles** at Eldon Insurance, vvast, Creation, and Bristol agencies laid the foundations: custom WordPress builds, CS-Cart and Drupal e-commerce, responsive HTML email campaigns, server monitoring, and speed optimisation across client portfolios.

I hold a degree from the **University of Plymouth** and have been writing code professionally since 2014.
